About Owner


Meet Raj D'souza, the Heart Behind Blue Star Coffee House

Raj D'souza is the visionary and passionate founder of Blue Star Coffee House. With a deep love for coffee and a commitment to creating a welcoming community space, Raj D'souza has brought a unique blend of warmth and excellence to the cafe.With a background in culinary arts, business, Raj D'souza has always prioritized quality and customer experience. Every detail, from the selection of premium coffee beans to the design of the cozy interiors, reflects his dedication to excellence and community.Raj D'souza believes that a great coffee house is more than just a place to get your caffeine fix. It's a community hub where people can gather, share ideas, and feel at home

About Workers

Meet Our Team

At Blue Star Coffee House, we believe that great coffee and exceptional service come from a passionate and dedicated team. Each member of our team brings their unique skills and personality to create a warm and inviting atmosphere for our customers.

Baristas Extraordinaire

Our baristas are the heart and soul of Blue Star Coffee House. Trained extensively in the art of coffee making, they not only craft the perfect cup but also ensure every customer feels welcome. With a deep knowledge of coffee beans, brewing techniques, and flavor profiles, they are always ready to recommend the perfect drink to suit your taste.

Culinary Creatives

Our kitchen staff, led by Sandeep Singh, takes pride in creating delicious and freshly prepared bakery items and snacks that complement our coffee perfectly. From the flaky croissants to the rich carrot cake, each item is made with high-quality ingredients and a lot of love. Their creativity and dedication ensure that our menu is always evolving with exciting new flavors.
